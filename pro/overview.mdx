Coreframe Pro gives you the complete design system toolkit — from granular color swatch editing and semantic token mapping to full component token control and developer-ready exports. If you're building a production-grade design system inside Figma, Pro removes every limitation of the free tier and hands you the controls you need to ship with confidence.

## Pro features

Everything in Pro is designed to take your token architecture and component library from a starting point to a fully structured, export-ready system.

- ✓ Individual color swatch editing
- ✓ Semantic token mapping
- ✓ Component token mapping
- ✓ Advanced typography editing
- ✓ Unlock all Pro components
- ✓ Custom spacing & semantic mapping
- ✓ Export to Tailwind theme (beta)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the purchase page">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Pro modal in Coreframe">
    Inside Figma, open the Coreframe plugin and click the **crown icon** in the top-right corner of the plugin panel to open the Pro activation screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your license key">
    Paste or type your license key into the activation field exactly as it appears in your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Activate">
    Hit **Activate** — your key is validated and Pro features unlock immediately. No restart required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your license key can be used on multiple Figma files. The key is stored locally in your Figma client and validated in the background.
</Note>
