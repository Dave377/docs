After purchasing Coreframe Pro, activating your license takes less than a minute — you enter your key directly inside the Figma plugin and Pro features unlock on the spot. This page walks you through every step of the activation process and explains how validation and offline access work.

## Activate your license

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Coreframe in Figma">
    Launch the Coreframe plugin from the Figma plugin menu in any file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the crown icon">
    In the top-right corner of the plugin panel, click the **crown (Pro) icon** to open the Pro modal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your license key">
    In the Pro modal, paste your license key into the input field. Make sure there are no leading or trailing spaces.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Activate License">
    Press the **Activate License** button. Coreframe will send your key to the validation server and confirm your Pro status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using Pro features">
    Once your key is validated, all Pro features unlock immediately in the current session — no restart or reload required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Where to find your license key

Your license key is delivered by email right after you complete your purchase on LemonSqueezy. Search your inbox for an email from LemonSqueezy with the subject line referencing your Coreframe order. If the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder — transactional mail from payment processors occasionally gets filtered. You can also retrieve your key at any time by logging into your LemonSqueezy customer account and viewing your order history.

## License validation

When you click **Activate License**, Coreframe sends your key to a secure validation server to confirm it is genuine and active. Once the check passes, the result is cached locally for 24 hours. This means the plugin continues to work normally even if you go offline — as long as the cache hasn't expired, Coreframe won't need a network connection to confirm your Pro status. After 24 hours, the plugin silently re-validates in the background the next time you use it online.

<Warning>
  Keep your license key safe — treat it like a password. If you lose it, contact support through your LemonSqueezy order page to retrieve or reissue it.
</Warning>

<Note>
  Your license and activation ID are stored in Figma's local storage on your machine. Switching computers requires re-entering your key on the new machine.
</Note>
