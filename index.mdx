## Get Started in Four Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the Plugin">
    Open Figma, search for **Coreframe** in the plugin marketplace, and install it to your workspace. Launch it from the **Plugins** menu in any Figma file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Your Tokens">
    Define your brand's colors, typography, spacing, and other design tokens inside Coreframe's token editor. You can start from a preset or build from scratch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate Components">
    With your tokens in place, click **Generate** to let Coreframe build a fully structured component library — buttons, inputs, cards, and more — directly in your Figma file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export for Development">
    Use Coreframe's developer export tools to output your tokens and components as CSS variables, JSON, or platform-specific code, ready to hand off to your engineering team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
